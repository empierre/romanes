Romanesque Corsica

  Built on a basilica plan, with very few openings, the Romanesque churches of Corsica surprise first the traveler whojudge them austere. Yet soon, they seduce, as they blend harmoniously with their natural setting. The reliefs which adorn enigmatics add to their charm a hint of mystery.
